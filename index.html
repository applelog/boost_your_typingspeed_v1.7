How to Play

Practice Mode

Type the given sentences. After completing a sentence, press Enter to move to the next one.

Alphabet Mode

Type the letter shown in the center to practice key positions.

Test Mode

You have 60 seconds to type a paragraph. Your WPM and typing level will be measured.

Game Mode

Type 20 words faster than the AI to win the race! The AI speed changes based on the level you choose.
